ABOUT ME

I am a CTO at a startup company (NIANTONG INTELLIGENCE) in Shanghai, China. Currently I am primarily working on Embedded Systems, Hardware Prototyping and physiological signals (especially EEG, EMG) processing.

I’m interested in transforming research outcomes into practical products. I have five years working experience on designing compact, low-power, wearable medical device and BCI（Brain computer interface）system. I led and developed the following products: eCon-Hand, iRecorder, eConAlpha, Myoelectric Prosthesis, iFES, iFocus and FPGA+USB based high density Ecog (electrocorticographic) acquisition device.

I got my Master’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My Master’s researches include Designing a Miniaturized Invasive Brain signal Acquisition System，Flexible Invasive Brain-Machine Interface Electrode and MEMS（Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems）sensor，supervised by Prof. Jingquan Liu at the EE-MEMS LAB.
